IA for news reprise:
=> originally meant as posts outlining a vision, with a nutty thought here and there and some experimental suggestions, but it reached a way broader audience than IAs, and a lot of people read it more like a a "state of things" (we are in the info biz, search) or authoratitative statement of where things are headed.

So, with this reprise, I want to split off the experiments and loose thoughts from the overview of the "state of things" -- so that it can serve as both a primer and something that'll get you thinking about possible future avenues)
