And commercial strategy should be based on getting people to feel like we're helping them make their neighborhoods better places, more fun places. Take a cue from Kickstarter, where it's almost addictive to give money to people because it's cool and you *want to be a part of it*. Authenticity. Good-natured fun. Being part of something bigger. Getting appreciation for giving. Excitement. *Not* the obligation to pay "because this stuff costs money, period". *Not* desperate pleas to support the fourth pillar of democracy either.
